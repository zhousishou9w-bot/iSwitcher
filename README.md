# iSwitcher
Support for the immersive music experience.

Apple version:
Open the Music app, play the music,then click the iSwitcher. You can switch songs without any accessories, and you don’t have to open your eyes, especially when you are resting.
Long-press the icon to open the Invitation page (before short-press or click). Click the <- button at the top to go back to the switch widget.

Android version:
Open music apps, such as YouTube Music or Spotify, then click the iSwitcher. You can switch songs without any accessories, and you don’t have to open your eyes, especially when you are resting.
